Specification and Execution of Policies for Grid Service Selection.We show how a standard Grid service architecturecan be improved by interposing a policy enforcementengine between a calling application and the relative clientstubs.Therefore, with our solution selection and invocationsare not hard-coded into client applications but (declaratively)defined and enforced outside the clients; thereforethey can be (de)activated and modified on-line.Our policiesare specified using the PDL language which supportsspecification of preferences and prohibitions in the routingof remote invocations to Web services.
