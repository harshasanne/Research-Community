Towards Aspect-based Modeling of Self-Healing Web Services.The dynamic nature of the business world shows the continuous pressure to reduce expenses, increase revenues, and remain competitive. This calls for a rapid reaction to the market trends, a rapid handling of user needs, and a rapid understanding of forthcoming challenges. To support businesses in the process of reaching these goals, Web services need to be selfaware of the environment in which they operate and self-healing so they can recover to normal levels of operation after disturbances.
