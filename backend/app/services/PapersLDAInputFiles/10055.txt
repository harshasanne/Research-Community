Supporting Intercultural Collaboration: An Agent Coordination Workflow Model.Workflow management has already been worldwide accepted due to its capability to make organizations model and control work processes effectively and efficiently in a distributed environment. Currently, most of the workflow management systems (WfMSs) are lack of a coordination mechanism across different organizations. In this paper, we first propose an intercultural collaboration workflow process definition and then propose an agent coordination workflow model based on the consideration of cultural factors in intercultural collaboration work process modeling.
